Basic Info 


Q: What do I need to do to become an official club member?
        0. Talk to coach (by going to a practice) and obtain his approval (from trying out). Once coach approves you, follow the following steps:
	1. Pay the $30/quarter thru venmo @uciboxing or in Cash to Treasurer Tricia.
	2. Complete the Club's signup form (you don't have to submit again if you have completed last year)
	3. Go to the Arc's website and complete the 3 steps 
	(*** Attention: you have to signup with your UCINetID, don't sign up as a guest!!!***)
	- Step1: Pay$15 registration fee (once per year for UCI)
	- Step2: Create Account and Register: go to Boxing Club and click Register and follow the instructions.
	After complete all the steps, you will receive a free boxing club T-shirt and hat.

Q: When is the club fee due?
	- First Practice is free, but fee need to be paid by the 2nd or the 3rd practice

Q: What gear do I need?
	- For Practice: Wraps, boxing gloves (men's 16-18oz, women's 12-14oz), jump rope and shoes (boxing or running shoes are good)
	- For Sparring (In addition): Headgear, mouthpiece, and cup for men or chest plate for women.
	- Any types of gears can be bought from Coach Tyrone

Q: What is the training schedule?
	- On Campus: Monday/ Thursday 8:30pm-10: 30pm in the Arc's Training Zone (Technique/Conditioning)
	- Off-Campus: Sundays, generally 2:00pm-4: 30 pm, might change up to Coach's schedule, check Facebook for weekly schedule

Q: Carpooling available for events?
	- Yes, carpooling option is available for Sunday Practice, social bonding events. There will be a signup sheet for every event, so please check and signup for carpool if you need one.

Q: Is this beginner friendly?
	Right now. We no longer accept beginners. There are beginner classes at the beginning of every quarter taught by coach Tyrone also. Those will be good preperation for club level boxing. Please visit: www.campusrec.uci.edu/classes/martial/index.asp for more info.
