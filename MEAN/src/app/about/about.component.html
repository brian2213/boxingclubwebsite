HOW TO JOIN

It’s important that you complete all the steps below. Please do not hesitate to let any of the board members know if you encounter any difficulties.

  • Step 0: You would need to obtain approval from coach to join the club (either through tryouts or completion of beginner’s boxing class). Go to a practice or send us an email for more details.
  • Step 1: Pay the $30/quarter Boxing Club membership fee either through the club’s Venmo account (@uciboxing) or in cash to our treasurer Malek.
  • Step 2: UCI CLUB SPORTS REGISTRATION (Complete the steps below):
    • i. Register through Campus Recreation to pay the $15 administration fee. Link Here
    • ii. Create your player profile on Do Sports Easy. Each member must complete every section completely and upload requested documents.
    • iii. Every member will need to be approved by Club Sport administration prior to competing in any competition.
    • iv. Complete an ImPACT baseline test at prior to the their first competition (if you have already been baseline tested, you will not need to retake the test unless you received a concussion previously). Schedules for baseline test will be up later.
  • Step 3: Complete the club's Emergeny Contact Form.
