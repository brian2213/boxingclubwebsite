Frequently Asked Questions

Q: When is club fee due?

First practice is free, but fee needs to be paid by the 2nd or 3rd practice.

Q: What gear do I need?

For Practice: Wraps, boxing gloves (men’s 16-18 oz, women’s 12-14 oz), jump rope and shoes (boxing or running shoes are good).

For Sparring (In addition): Headgear, mouthpiece, and cup for men or chest plate for women.

Gears can be bought online from Amazon or the manufacturer’s website (do some research before purchasing). Gears can also be bought at a discounted price from coach Tyrone.

Q: Carpooling available for events?

Yes, carpooling option is available for Sunday Practice, social bonding events. There will be a signup sheet for every event, so please check our Facebook page or event page and sign up for carpool if you need one.

Q: Is this beginner’s friendly?

Unfortunately, we cannot accept beginners. If you are a beginner, you would need to complete the Beginner’s Boxing Class (http://www.campusrec.uci.edu/classes/martial/index.asp).
